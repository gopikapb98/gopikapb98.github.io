About Me

I am a computational and applied mathematician pursuing my doctoral research under the mentorship of Dr. Nagaiah Chamakuri. My work focuses on creating innovative algorithms and mathematical models to tackle complex real-world problems, with a special interest in mathematical biology. Dedicated to interdisciplinary collaboration and STEM advocacy, I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of mathematical knowledge and its applications.
